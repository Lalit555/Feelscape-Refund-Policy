Refund Policy

At Feelscale, we aim to provide a smooth and rewarding experience for our users. Whether you're purchasing coins or upgrading to premium, our systems are designed for fast and secure transactions. However, in rare cases, if a transaction fails to deliver what was promised, we are here to help.

Eligibility for Refund

Refunds will only be considered under the following circumstances:

  • The user was charged, but did not receive coins for the transaction.
  • The user purchased a premium membership but the upgrade was not applied to their account.

Required Information

To process a refund request, the user must provide:

  • Payment screenshot clearly showing the amount, time, and Razorpay transaction ID.
  • Razorpay Payment ID (e.g., pay_XXXXXXXX).
  • The registered email or phone number used in the app.
  • Details about the issue and the package they tried to purchase.

All refund requests must be emailed to feelscale.help@gmail.com.

Review and Approval

Upon receiving the necessary details, our support team will review your request. If the claim is found valid, we will initiate the refund within 5–7 business days.

Please note:

  • We reserve the right to approve or deny any refund request based on our internal verification.
  • Refunds will only be issued to the original source of payment.
  • We will not entertain refunds if the user has received the services (coins or premium upgrade) as described.

Non-Refundable Scenarios

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • User changed their mind after purchasing.
  • User was unable to use the service due to device or connectivity issues.
  • User attempted to abuse or manipulate the point/premium system.
  • User’s account is under investigation or has been banned for violating our policies.

International Transactions

If you are making a payment from outside India, Razorpay will handle currency conversion automatically. Refunds, if issued, will be processed in INR and converted back to your local currency by your card provider or bank, based on their exchange rates.

Contact Us

For all refund-related queries or concerns, please contact us at:
Email: feelscale.help@gmail.com

We value your trust and will do our best to ensure a fair resolution.

This refund policy is effective as of May 10, 2025. Feelscale reserves the right to modify or update this policy at any time without prior notice.
